# Henric Content Guidelines

Swedish copy, messaging strategy, and content patterns for the landing page.

## Brand positioning

**One-liner:** "Legora för fastighetsförvaltning"

**Value proposition:** AI-assistenten som hjälper svenska fastighetsförvaltare att spara tid på admin—fungerar i Vitec, Momentum, Incit Xpand, plus Word, Outlook och Excel.

**Core message:** Not replacement, but augmentation. Henric makes property managers better at their job, not threatened by automation.

## Headline patterns

### Transformation-focused (recommended)

| Swedish | English translation |
|---------|---------------------|
| Mindre admin. Mer förvaltning. | Less admin. More management. |
| Fastighetsförvaltning, förenklad. | Property management, simplified. |
| Ditt dagliga arbete, utan krångel. | Your daily work, without hassle. |
| Framtidens fastighetsförvaltning | The future of property management |

**Structure:** Promise transformation in 4-6 words. Avoid feature descriptions.

### Subheadline examples

- "AI som fungerar i Vitec, Momentum och Incit Xpand—plus Word, Outlook och Excel."
- "Spara timmar varje vecka på dokumentation, mejl och avtalsanalys."
- "Din AI-assistent för allt administrativt arbete."

## Trust signals

- Vitec logo

### Credibility statements

- "Svenskt team. Vi följer GDPR."
- "Byggt av fastighetsexperter."
- "Integrerat med systemen du redan använder."

### When you have customers

- Quote format: "Henric sparar mig minst 2 timmar varje dag." — [Name], [Role] på [Company]
- Quantified results: "X timmar sparade per vecka"
- "80% av användarna är aktiva dagligen"

## Swedish business culture notes

### Do

- Use partnership language: "Vi bygger detta tillsammans med våra användare"
- Provide named contact: "Frågor? Mejla Buddy på buddy@henricproperty.com"
- Embrace lagom (moderation): balanced claims, no hyperbole
- Be transparent and direct

### Avoid

- Aggressive urgency: "Act now!", countdown timers
- Over-promising: "Revolutionary", "Game-changing"
- Vendor language: Use partner/collaborator framing
- Generic AI claims: "AI-powered" means nothing

## Writing style

### Reading level
Write at 5th-7th grade level. Simple words, short sentences. Avoid jargon.

### Tone
- Professional but warm
- Confident but not arrogant
- Direct but not aggressive
- Helpful, not salesy

### Formatting
- Use active voice
- Lead with benefits, not features
- One idea per sentence
- Break up long paragraphs
